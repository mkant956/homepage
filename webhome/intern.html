Internship

evive - Agilo Technologies

Worked as an Intern in Agilo Technologies’ Arduino based embedded prototyping platform evive. My work here was to develop various use cases for the evive. Some of them are –

  • Obstacle avoiding robot with integrated IMU which helped robot to follow particular path to reach its destination avoiding the inbetween obstacle.

  • Smartphone controlled skateboard using bluetooth module to control via smartphone and proximity sensor to prevent accidental collision

  • Home automation system for smart homes with a digital password protected door lock, smartphone controlled home appliances, and capable of detecting human presence and light up the rooms accordingly

  • Various IoT Applications like

    • Chat Bot – chatting with two different evive via WiFi

    • Twitter Bot – Tweet to ypur personal Twitter account via evive

    • Plant Monitoring system – to monitor moisture and temperature of plant environment and water them accordingly

  • Made detailed instructions tutorial for these projects as well on Instructables, Hackster.io etc.
